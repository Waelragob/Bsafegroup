Meet Our Leadership

BSAFE GROUP is led by a team of experienced professionals who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project we undertake. Our leadership team has over 60 years of combined experience in construction engineering, digital transformation, logistics optimization, and business management.

We are committed to delivering excellence in every aspect of our business, and our team's diverse skillset enables us to provide comprehensive solutions to our clients' most complex challenges.

1

CEO & Founder

Ahmed Al-Saeed is the founder and CEO of BSAFE GROUP. With over 20 years of experience in engineering management, Ahmed has led numerous large-scale infrastructure projects across the Middle East. He brings a strategic vision and hands-on approach to every project, ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety.

His passion for innovation and continuous improvement has driven the company's growth and established BSAFE GROUP as a leader in integrated solutions for construction, digital transformation, and logistics management.
